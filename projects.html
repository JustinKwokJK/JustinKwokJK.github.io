TEJ Final Project: Arduino Car

For my TEJ Final Project, I created a car using an Arduino. This car is equipped with multiple features such as a horn, speed adjustment, and working signals. It was a challenging but rewarding experience to combine hardware and software to create a functional vehicle.

SHAD Project: F.A.R.M.E.R.S

Team: Djemima B, Adam K, Justin K, Parinika D, Benifier M, Shayne Y

During my SHAD experience, I worked on a project called F.A.R.M.E.R.S, where we aimed to address global energy use issues. We developed an eco-friendly farm that integrates sustainable technologies and serves as a learning center for both new and experienced farmers. This project helped me understand the importance of sustainability and innovation in solving real-world problems.
